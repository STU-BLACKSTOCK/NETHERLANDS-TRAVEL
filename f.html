Efteling Theme Park



Enter a world of magic and adventure at Efteling, one of Europe’s oldest and most beloved theme parks. With fairytale forests, thrilling rides, and enchanting shows, it’s perfect for families and thrill-seekers alike. Efteling’s beautifully landscaped grounds and Dutch folklore-inspired attractions provide an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages.

Opening Period: Year-round

Location: Europalaan 1, 5171 KW Kaatsheuvel, Netherlands
