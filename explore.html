AMSTERDAM

A city of storybook canals and timeless charm, where art, history, and vibrant nightlife meet. From tulips and bicycles to world-famous museums, every corner of Amsterdam invites discovery and delight.

ROTTERDAM

A bold city of modern architecture and innovation, where sleek skyscrapers rise along the Maas River. Known for its cutting-edge design, lively nightlife, and bustling port, Rotterdam exudes a cool, forward-thinking energy.

THE HAGUE

The seat of Dutch royalty and international justice, The Hague blends grandeur and tranquility. With sandy beaches, historic palaces, and embassies, it’s a refined city where politics meets seaside charm.

UTRECHT

A cozy, canal-lined gem with medieval charm, Utrecht is alive with student energy and historic beauty. Its iconic Dom Tower, vibrant cafes, and scenic canals make it a perfect blend of history and liveliness.

HAARLEM

Known as the "little Amsterdam," Haarlem is rich in culture and charm, with stunning architecture, cozy cafes, and vibrant flower markets. Its museums and scenic canals make it a hidden gem in the heart of the Netherlands.

DELFT

A picturesque city of canals and cobblestones, Delft is the birthplace of Vermeer and famous for its iconic blue pottery. With historic churches and charming streets, it’s a blend of art, tradition, and timeless beauty.
