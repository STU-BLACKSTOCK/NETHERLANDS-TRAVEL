THE HAGUE

Places to Visit!

Here are some placed you can visit in The Hague

Peace Palaces

The Peace Palace in The Hague is a majestic symbol of international justice and diplomacy. Home to the International Court of Justice and the Permanent Court of Arbitration, this grand building represents the global pursuit of peace. Visitors can admire its impressive Neo-Renaissance architecture, tour its beautiful gardens, and learn about the building’s history in fostering conflict resolution. With its iconic bell tower and intricate design, the Peace Palace stands as a powerful reminder of the importance of dialogue and cooperation among nations.

Museum Beelden aan Zee

Museum Beelden aan Zee in Scheveningen is a unique seaside museum dedicated to modern and contemporary sculpture. Nestled in the sand dunes along the North Sea, it houses an extensive collection of intriguing works by Dutch and international artists. The museum’s light-filled galleries and outdoor sculpture garden create a serene atmosphere where art and nature blend beautifully. With changing exhibitions and its picturesque coastal setting, Museum Beelden aan Zee offers a refreshing, immersive art experience by the sea.

De Pier in Scheveningen

De Pier in Scheveningen is a lively and iconic spot along the Dutch coast, offering fun, food, and spectacular views over the North Sea. Stroll along its multi-level boardwalk to find trendy food stalls, shops, and cozy lounges, or take a ride on the Ferris wheel for panoramic ocean and beach vistas. For thrill-seekers, there's bungee jumping and a zipline that zips right over the water. With year-round activities and a vibrant atmosphere, De Pier is a must-visit destination for seaside adventure and relaxation.
