Tulip Festival



Celebrate spring at the Netherlands' famous Tulip Festival, a vibrant display of tulips across gardens and fields. From March to May, millions of tulips bloom, creating colorful landscapes in places like Keukenhof Gardens. The festival attracts flower lovers from around the world, offering tours, events, and photo opportunities in the heart of Dutch tulip country.

Opening Period: Late March to Mid-May

Location: Various locations, including Keukenhof Gardens
