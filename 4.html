UTRECHT

Places to Visit!

Here are some placed you can visit in Utrecht

Dom Tower

The Dom Tower in Utrecht is the tallest and most famous church tower in the Netherlands, standing proudly as a symbol of the city’s rich history. Climb its 465 steps for stunning panoramic views over Utrecht and beyond, or admire its intricate Gothic architecture from below. Built in the 14th century, the tower is part of the historic Dom Church, and it has survived centuries of storms and changes. A visit to the Dom Tower is a journey into Utrecht’s medieval past and an unforgettable experience for history and architecture lovers alike.

DOMunder

DOMunder in Utrecht offers a fascinating underground journey through the city's rich history. Explore the hidden layers beneath the Dom Square, where ancient Roman, medieval, and early modern artifacts are unearthed. Using interactive displays and guided tours, visitors can experience the evolution of Utrecht from its Roman roots to its medieval glory. This unique archaeological adventure brings the city’s past to life, offering a deeper connection to Utrecht’s heritage. DOMunder is a must-visit for history enthusiasts looking to discover the layers of time beneath this vibrant city.

Centraal Museum

The Centraal Museum in Utrecht is a cultural gem that showcases the city’s artistic and historical heritage. Housing a diverse collection of art, from medieval works to contemporary pieces, the museum offers something for every art lover. Highlights include the works of Utrecht’s own Old Masters, as well as modern design and fashion exhibits. With its beautifully restored building and engaging exhibitions, the Centraal Museum provides an enriching experience for visitors looking to explore the rich cultural tapestry of Utrecht.
